Welcome to Nicole Bakes Cakes!

Chef Nicole is a professional pastry chef based in Southern California with over 10 years of experience in cakes, cupcakes and many other sweet treats. She loves making people happy with the cakes she makes: Nicole's goal is to make all of your events (weddings, showers, birthday parties, etc) unforgettable and unique!

Chef Nicole only uses the highest quality ingredients, and can bake around just about any special dietary or allergic needs. She applies lots of care and love to every creation and uses her incredible attention for detail to bring your cake dreams to reality!

Make your next special event memorable with a unique, rich and sweet creation (or creations!) with Chef Nicole.

Chef Nicole's work was recently featured on a popular event planning blog, "Hostess with the Mostest." The featured event was a Monsters University themed kids birthday party extravaganza, with Nicole's work serving as the centerpiece, as well as providing unique and fun finger sweets and treats for both kids and adults.
